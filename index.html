About me

Designing for the edge case.

Throughout my academic trajectory, I've been drawn to exploring the human-centred side of computing and using it to uplift underserved communities i.e. edge cases.

Currently, I'm applying for Master's programs in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) with a plan to work alongside impact-driven organisations after. The following are my domains of interest within HCI research:

  • Accessibility design

  • Social computing

  • Ubiquitous computing

More specifically, I want to explore the intersection of accessibility design and social computing, using the latter as input for the former. I believe this holds significant weight for the Next Billion Users (NBU) initiative. I hope to meaningfully contribute to this area in the near future - please let me know of any open opportunities!

Outside of academics, I'm a competent photographer, a less competent swimmer and a serial Youtube watcher.
