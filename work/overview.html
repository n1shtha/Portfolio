Selected works

Below are three instrumental endeavours that are centred around social data or impact. These are individual and group projects that range from technological solutions to UI/UX case studies. To explore more work, please check out the Archives for experiments in interfaces, photography and design.

Transforming donation systems and improving transparency by removing the role of middle-men organisations. This permissioned platform facilitates purpose-specific donations, real-time tracking, and anonymity.

Mapping anonymity in
Partition literature

An interactive exploration of unnamed characters across literature from the 1947 India-Pakistan Partition. These maps represent data about these characters in zones of religious conflict, along with gender and space-modality views.
